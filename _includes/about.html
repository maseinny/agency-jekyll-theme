About

  • Strawberry Patch Puzzles started from a birthday gift idea for a special someone who loves puzzles and scavenger hunts.

  • It grew from a way to make a physical gift that wasn't exciting enough become more interesting by incorporating it into a scavenger hunt, to taking on a life of it's own and becoming the main gift.

  • Clues were put up online, around the house, and around the city, sending the birthday boy on a quest to find his gift as well as when and where to meet for dinner that night.

  • The clues are varied to keep things interesting - no 2 clues are the same. The puzzle employs things like custom web pages, maps, social media, text messages, music, language, luggage locks, sound modules and an Arduino board, riddles, and a nonogram.
